Clients Report

The website for Rolling Slow has left me very impressed. The movements within the logo help the website to come alive and almost interact with our customers. I really enjoyed the yellow theme for the overall design, it helps coordinate the entire design of the webpage. The way the icons up top enlarge and tint to a yellow is very helpful for the user to navigate in a more efficient way. One more thing I found that I was drawn to was how the images for our products fit the aesthetic of the website design in a very fluid way. The color scheme flows well between each image and emphasizes our vibe. One thing I had noticed was that the employees were not included in the About Us page on the mobile site. I would love to find a way to include them into that page, as we are a close-knit company. I would also really like to see a line for email addresses on the check out page. This way, we can compile an email list of clients to send promotional materials to, and we can update them on the status of their orders. Overall, I found my eyes glued to the webpage. The design fits our target audience and captures our companies essence. I am excited to see where Jordan can take this website next.
